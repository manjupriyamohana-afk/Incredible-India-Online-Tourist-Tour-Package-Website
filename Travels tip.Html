    Tips for Tourists

  1. Carry valid ID proof
  2. Respect local culture
  3. Drink safe water
  4. Keep emergency contacts

Mistakes to Avoid During a Tour

  1. Not planning the trip properly
  2. Ignoring weather conditions
  3. Overpacking or underpacking luggage
  4. Not carrying important documents
  5. Ignoring local culture and rules
  6. Poor budget planning
  7. Skipping travel insurance
  8. Not saving emergency contact numbers
  9. Trying to cover too many places in less time
  10. Not drinking safe water or eating hygienic food
